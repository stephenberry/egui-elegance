FAQ — standard
How do I invite teammates to my workspace?

Open Settings ▸ Members and click Invite. You can paste a list of emails or share a signup link scoped to a specific role.

Invitations expire after 7 days. Re-sending a link generates a fresh token; older links become invalid immediately.

What happens when I archive a project?

Archived projects are hidden from the sidebar and read-only. You can restore them from the archive list at any time within 90 days.

Is there an API for bulk imports?

Yes. See the Imports API reference. Rate limits apply per workspace; contact support if you need a larger quota.

Can I use SSO with a custom IdP?

SAML 2.0 and OIDC are supported on the Business plan and above. SCIM provisioning is in private beta.
